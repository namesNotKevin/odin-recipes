Rice (this page is a joke)

Description

Rice is an versatile food that can be eaten with everything, from steaks to omelettes. This recipe will show you how to cook rice with the most common way, with a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Rice (2 cups)
  • Water (2 cups)

Steps

  1. Take 2 cups of uncooked rice and pour into a strainer
  2. Rinse the rice over a sink
  3. Pour rice into rice cooker along with 2 cups of water
  4. Press start button
